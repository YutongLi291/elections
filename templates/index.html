CSSS Elections

Welcome to the Computer Science Student Society's online election system.

To vote you will need to be a Computer Science student, or be intending to go into Computer Science and have taken CPSC 110 and CPSC 121 in the past year.

If you don't have a CS account, you can get one from getacct. If you run into issues logging in, please try running getacct or using a desktop browser. Links opened in the Messenger web view are known to break login.
